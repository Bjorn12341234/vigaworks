Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Who we are

Viga Works AB ("we", "us") is a Swedish company that develops and publishes mobile games. Our registered address is Hålekärrsgatan 8, 414 66 Göteborg, Sweden. Org nr: 559534-5009.

Summary

We respect your privacy. Here's the short version:

  • Our games do not require an account.
  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not use third-party tracking by default.
  • If a game uses analytics, ads, or crash reporting, we explain it clearly below.

What data we collect

By default, our games do not collect personal data. If a specific game collects data (for example, through analytics, ads, or crash reporting), the details are listed in the game-specific disclosures section below.

When we might collect data

We may collect limited data in the following cases:

  • Crash reporting: If enabled for a game, we may collect anonymous crash logs to fix bugs. This includes device model, OS version, and stack traces. No personally identifiable information is included.
  • Analytics: If enabled for a game, we may collect anonymous usage data (such as session length and screens visited) to improve the game experience.
  • Ads: If a game displays ads, the ad provider may collect data according to their own privacy policy. We will link to the relevant provider's policy in the game-specific disclosures.
  • Support emails: If you email us, we store your message and email address to respond to your request. We do not use this data for marketing.

Purpose and legal basis

Any data we collect is used to:

  • Fix bugs and improve game stability (legitimate interest)
  • Understand how games are used so we can improve them (legitimate interest)
  • Display ads where applicable (consent, where required)
  • Respond to support requests (contractual necessity)

Data retention

  • Crash logs: Deleted within 90 days.
  • Analytics data: Aggregated and anonymized. Raw data deleted within 90 days.
  • Support emails: Kept for up to 12 months after the last reply, then deleted.
  • Ad data: Managed by the ad provider according to their retention policy.

Your choices

  • You can delete a game and all its local data at any time by uninstalling it.
  • Where ads require consent, you can opt out through the game's settings or your device's ad settings.
  • You can request deletion of any support correspondence by emailing support@vigaworks.com.

Children's privacy

Our games are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

Third-party services

If a game uses third-party services (ad networks, analytics providers), those services have their own privacy policies. We will list them in the game-specific disclosures below when applicable.
