Hnefatafl

Viking board game Android In development

About

Hnefatafl is an ancient Norse strategy game — sometimes called "Viking chess." One side defends the king, the other attacks. Simple rules, deep tactics.

We're building a clean, mobile-friendly version you can play against the computer or pass-and-play with a friend.

Status

Currently in development. We'll update this page when the game is available on Google Play.
